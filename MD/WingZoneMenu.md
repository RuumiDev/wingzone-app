# Wing Zone Menu

## 🔥 Flavor Selection
*Choose your flavor based on the Heat Index!*

### Traditional Buffalo Flavors
* **Buffalo Wing** (🌶️🌶️🌶️🌶️): A classic buffalo sauce is sharp, rich and hot. Tangy, salty and creamy texture.
* **Sriracha Hot Chilli** (🌶️🌶️🌶️🌶️): Tangy, smoky sweet chili flavor that adds zip and bam to savory dishes.

### Wing Zone Favorites
* **Soul of Seoul** (🌶️🌶️🌶️): Spicy Korean BBQ with a hint of typical Korean Rice Flavor.
* **Garlic Parm** (🌶️🌶️): A garlic love affair with zesty Buffalo flavor, parmesan and a kiss of pepper.
* **Mambo Sauce** (🌶️🌶️): A bold and flavorful taste that's a balance of sweet and tangy. Slight heat, not overwhelming.

### Sweet With No Heat
* **Sweet Samurai**: Asian traditionalists love this glaze of soy, honey, ginger and teriyaki flavor.
* **Honey Q**: Sweet honey and smoky BBQ hook up to deliver Great Southern flavor.

### Dry Rub Flavors
* **Blackened Voodoo** (🌶️): Sizzling wings with the dry rub Cajun flavors of New Orleans.
* **Lemon Pepper**: Deliciously smooth, new dry rubs of lemon pepper leaving wings crispy.

### Sweet & Spicy Flavors
* **Louisiana Smoked** (🌶️🌶️): Smokey BBQ enhanced with savory spices & hint of citrus.
* **Spicy Alabama** (🌶️🌶️): Gentle heat, sweet surrender and Cheesy blend from the Great South.
* **Tokyo Dragon** (🌶️🌶️): Hot honey meets classic Teriyaki for an experience of ginger, soy and heat.
* **Thai Chili** (🌶️🌶️): Far east fusion of spicy Thai plus sweet & sour.
* **Sweet Bombom** (🌶️🌶️🌶️): A slightly spicy kick, on the milder side with sweetness from sweet chili sauce.
* **Smokin Q** (🌶️🌶️🌶️): BBQ smokehouse flavor with a serious cayenne kick.
